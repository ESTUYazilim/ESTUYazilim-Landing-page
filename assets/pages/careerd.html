CareerD+

In our CareerD+ events, we aim to build a bridge between the industry and students by bringing together experts from the industry with our members.

In this way, our members have the opportunity to learn about business life while they are still students, to ask questions and find answers one-on-one, to get to know the areas they want to focus on better, and to catch various internship and job opportunities.
